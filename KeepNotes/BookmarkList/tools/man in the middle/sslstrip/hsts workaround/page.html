Strict-Transport-Security header prevents the original HTTP-to-HTTPS redirection from happening, making use of SSLStrip more difficult. Some options to help defeat HSTS:

SSLStrip2 (also integrated in bettercap by default unless --no-sslstrip specified)

MitM Change Domain
HSTS sometimes works based off of pre-defined hostnames etc:
www.facebook.com <- HSTS enabled
wwww.facebook.com <- HSTS NOT enabled, but still directs to facebook.com
