Phase 5: Maintenance and Operations

Phase 5.1: Conduct Operational Management Reviews
There  needs  to  be  a  process  in  place  which  details  how  the  oper-ational side of both the application and infrastructure is managed.

Phase 5.2: Conduct Periodic Health Checks
Monthly  or  quarterly  health  checks  should  be  performed  on  both  the  application  and  infrastructure  to  ensure  no  new  security  risks  have been introduced and that the level of security is still intact.

Phase 5.3: Ensure Change Verification
After  every  change  has  been  approved  and  tested  in  the  QA  envi-ronment  and  deployed  into  the  production  environment,  it  is  vital  that the change is checked to ensure that the level of security has not been affected by the change. This should be integrated into the change management process.
