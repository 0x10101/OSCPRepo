While on a pentest, how can you identify the cipher in use?

Is it a hash?
Tools like hash-identifier will evaluate a hash by length and format and give recommendations

Is it even encrypted?



Look at data size:
  Not evenly divisible by 8? Increased likelyhood to be a stream cipher
  Always divisible by 16: Increased likelyhood for block cipher 128-bit block (AES)
  Sometimes divisibly by 16, always by 8: 64-bit block size (DES/3DES)
Look for documentation from vendor, call engineering support, etc
