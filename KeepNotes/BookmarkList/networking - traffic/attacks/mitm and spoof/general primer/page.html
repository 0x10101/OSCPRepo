Note that some spoofing techniques may not be effective until clients reload their cache.
Also note that when exiting spoofing, ensure that clients will still have a route for traffic (otherwise there will be a DoS)

Always try to specifically target clients on a network by using MAC or IP address. The louder you are, the more likely you are to be detected. A computer is not necessarily faster than a dedicated routing/switching device.

It will almost always be usefil to log to a pcap or capture traffic another way in order to troubleshoot or examine if attacks were carried out correctly.
