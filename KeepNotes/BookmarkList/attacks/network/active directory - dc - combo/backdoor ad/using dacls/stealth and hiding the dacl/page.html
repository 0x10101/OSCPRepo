Effectifvely hiding DACLs requires two steps:
  • Change the object owner from "Domain Admins" to the attacker account
  • Add a new explicit ACE, denying the "Everyone" principal the "Read Permissions" privilege

Hiding a principal from defendes requires three steps:
  • Change the principal owner to itself or another controled principal
  • Grant explicit ocntrol of the principal to either itself, or another controlled principal
  • On the OU containing your hidden principal, deny the "List Contents" privilege to "Everyone"
