Check for vulnerabilities in subdomains
Subdomains are not technically 'cross' origin (assuming origin is the entire domain), but allow an attacker to chain together vulnerabilities that may be present in a domain to a subdomain and then accross to another domain.
eg: hacking fortnite XSS in ut2004stats.epicgames.com can make requests to account.epicgames.com

This could be considered a misconfiguration for 'overly permissive' CORS policy, eg: *.domain.com
